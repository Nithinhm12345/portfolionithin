Mini project

Title:Hybrid Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

Aim

In a hybrid power generation setup, we combine Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT) with solar panels to create a more reliable energy source. This system ensures continuous power generation, even when one source is less efficient due to weather conditions. During the day, solar panels capture sunlight and generate electricity, while the wind turbine harnesses wind energy at all times. At night or during cloudy weather, the turbine continues to produce power, balancing the system. This combination maximizes energy output and efficiency. It also helps reduce dependency on fossil fuels, making it an eco-friendly solution. Such a hybrid approach enhances energy stability and supports sustainable power needs.
